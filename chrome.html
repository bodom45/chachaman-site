// The Story

Who Is ChaChaMan.

DJ. Producer. Artist. Raised in New York, where open format isn't a style — it's survival. If the room moves, it's in the crate: classic house, hip-hop, R&B edits, Jersey club, and the hip-house lane in between.

Behind the decks, ChaChaMan reads rooms and builds energy. Behind the scenes, he's an entrepreneur specializing in digital marketing — and the owner of Area 51 Radio, a Texas music media platform putting independent artists in front of real audiences.

Music and marketing aren't two careers. They're one operating system — the same instinct for what makes people stop, listen, and move.
